Frequently asked questions

Where is my data stored?

Everything is stored locally on your device. There is no account and no server. If you enable iCloud, data syncs only to your own private iCloud — we never receive it.

How does interest work?

When adding a transaction you can enable simple or compound interest with a rate and period. Interest recalculates automatically as you record payments.

What is included in Pro?

Pro unlocks Cloud Backup, Export/Import, App Lock (PIN), and unlimited books. You can restore a previous purchase anytime from Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchases.

I forgot my PIN. What now?

For security, the PIN cannot be recovered. Deleting and reinstalling the app resets the lock, but this also clears local data — restore from your iCloud/exported backup afterwards.

How do I back up or move my data?

With Pro, use Settings → Cloud Backup, or Export Data to save a file you can Import on another device.

Which languages and currencies are supported?

The app is available in English and Bengali, and supports multiple currencies (change it in Settings).
