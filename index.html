Questions

Where is my data stored?

On your device only. There's no account and no server. Enable iCloud and it syncs to your own private iCloud — we never receive it.

How does interest work?

Add a rate and period when creating a transaction — simple or compound. It recalculates automatically as you record payments.

What's in Pro?

Cloud backup, PDF export, data export/import, App Lock (PIN), and unlimited books. The free tier includes up to two books.

I forgot my PIN.

For security the PIN can't be recovered. Reinstalling clears the lock but also local data — restore from your iCloud or exported backup afterwards.
