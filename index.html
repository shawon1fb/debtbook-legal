What Debt Book does

The ledger for money between people

We all lend and borrow — a friend short on cash, a family medical bill, a shopkeeper's running tab. Debt Book replaces the notebook (khata) and the memory with one clear, private ledger. You record who owes what, the app tracks interest and payments, and you always know exactly where things stand.

Inside the app

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Four core ideas power Debt Book — each one designed to remove a specific headache of tracking money by hand.

01 — INTEREST

Interest calculates itself

Set a simple or compound rate — daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly — and Debt Book recalculates the accrued interest automatically every time a payment comes in.

  • Simple or compound interest
  • Any period: daily to yearly
  • Recalculates on every payment
02 — ORGANIZATION

Personal & business, kept separate

Create unlimited books to split personal, family, and business ledgers. Each book keeps its own people, transactions, and totals — nothing gets mixed up.

  • Unlimited books with Pro
  • Switch books in one tap
  • Per-book lent/borrowed totals
03 — SEARCH

Find anything, instantly

Search across people, transactions, and books at once. Filter by date range to pull up exactly the record you need in seconds.

  • Search people, books & amounts
  • Date-range filtering
  • Results as you type
04 — PRIVACY

Locked down, by default

Everything is stored on your device using SwiftData — there is no account and no server. Add a PIN and auto-lock to keep the app closed to anyone else, even if they pick up your phone.

  • PIN lock + auto-lock
  • No account, no sign-up
  • Optional backup to your own iCloud

Questions

Good to know

Where is my data stored?

On your device only, using SwiftData. There's no account and no server. If you turn on iCloud backup (Pro), it syncs to your own private iCloud — we never receive it.

How does interest work?

Add a rate and period when creating a transaction — simple or compound. It recalculates automatically every time you record a payment.

What do I get with Pro?

Unlimited books, cloud backup & sync, PDF statements, and data export/import. The free tier includes up to two books, so you can try the app fully before upgrading.

Do I need an account?

No. Debt Book works immediately after install — no sign-up, no login. A profile name is optional and only used to label your own PDF statements.

I forgot my PIN — what now?

For security, PINs can't be recovered. Reinstalling clears the lock but also local data — restore from your iCloud backup or exported file afterward.
