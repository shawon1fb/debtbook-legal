FAQ

Good questions

Is my data private?

Completely. Everything is stored on your device — there is no account and no server. Optional iCloud backup stays in your own private iCloud account and never reaches us.

Does it calculate interest?

Yes. Choose simple or compound interest with any rate and period. It recalculates automatically every time you record a payment.

Can I use it for my shop's customer credit?

Absolutely — many shopkeepers use Debt Book as a digital khata/udhar book. Create a business book, add customers, and track dues with interest.

Is Debt Book free?

Yes, free to download and use. Debt Book Pro is an optional upgrade for cloud backup, PDF export, PIN lock and unlimited books.
